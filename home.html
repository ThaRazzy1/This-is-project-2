The Family Spot is a soulfood resturant where all the food is homemade and features a tastebud tickling sensation in every bite. These recipies will bring back fond memories for those who miss that "good ole southern cookin" a.k.a "home". Here at The Family Spot, we include a soup and salad bar to enjoy with your entree and a "soup of the day" for those with extra special taste buds. We also have a kids menu available for those hungry little youngins', as well as a take out menu for those people who are in a hurry and have to eat on the go. Catering service is also available for those who are interested. So come on in and sit a spell, because here at The Family Spot, everybody is family.
